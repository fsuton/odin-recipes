Degenerate Millionaire Streamers $2 Steak

It's ya boy baldie master

Ingredients

  • Cheapest pack of steaks from supermarket
  • Generic steak spice
  • A1 Steak sauce
  • 1 potato

Steps

  1. Heat up some oil in the pan
  2. Stab the potato few times with a fork
  3. Put the potato in the microwave for 8 minutes
  4. Place the steak in the heated pan
  5. Add spice
  6. Cook for 2 minutes per side, stab the steak with the fork few times
  7. Take out the steak, leave it to rest until potato is done cooking
  8. Serve with the potato and a generous ammount of A1 Steak sauce
  9. Enjoy
